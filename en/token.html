About EnergieMIND Token

EnergieMIND Token (ticker: ENM) is the Ethereum ERC-20 utility token for the EnergieMIND ecosystem. On-chain, the verified contract registers the name "EnergieMind" with symbol "ENM". It is designed for future coordination related to energy intelligence, mining heat utility participation, pilot programs, energy infrastructure incentives, and EnteleKRON mainnet alignment.

EnergieMIND Token (ENM) is documented on enm.network, separate from the EnergieMIND project website at energiemind.com, which covers technology and infrastructure.
