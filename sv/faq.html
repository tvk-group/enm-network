What is EnergieMIND?
EnergieMIND (ticker: ENM) is the utility token of the EnergieMIND energy intelligence ecosystem. It is an ERC-20 token on Ethereum Mainnet, designed for future ecosystem coordination related to energy intelligence, mining heat participation, pilot programs, and EnteleKRON mainnet alignment. ENM is a utility token — not an investment product.
What are the official EnergieMIND domains?
energiemind.com — main corporate/project website; energiemind.io — technology platform, dashboard, and app; energiemind.network — partners, nodes, miner-heaters, and energy network; energiemind.org — sustainability, research, and public mission; energiemind.shop — shop for miners, miner-heaters, solar, and energy products; enm.network — EnergieMIND (ENM), presale, and token documentation (this site).
How does EnergieMIND relate to EnteleKRON, TVK Group, and TVK Labs?
EnergieMIND (ENM) is the energy-intelligence utility token documented on enm.network. EnteleKRON is the planned mainnet alignment ecosystem referenced in the ENM roadmap — alignment is a technical direction, not a guarantee of migration, value, or return. TVK Group is the corporate project host (energiemind.com). TVK Labs supports technology development (energiemind.io). The investor portal workflow is similar to EnteleKRON and SOVRA protocol portals, but those are separate projects. Nothing on enm.network presents ENM as an investment product. Etherscan
What is the difference between enm.network and energiemind.com?
enm.network is dedicated to EnergieMIND (ENM): contract, tokenomics, presale, investor portal, and risk disclosures. energiemind.com is the main corporate and project website. Other ecosystem functions use energiemind.io, energiemind.network, energiemind.org, and energiemind.shop.
