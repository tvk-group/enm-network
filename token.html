About ENM

ENM (EnergieMind) is the Ethereum ERC-20 utility token for the EnergieMIND ecosystem. It is designed for future coordination related to energy intelligence, mining heat utility participation, pilot programs, energy infrastructure incentives, and EnteleKRON mainnet alignment.

ENM is separate from the EnergieMIND project website at energiemind.com, which covers technology and infrastructure. This site documents the token layer only.
