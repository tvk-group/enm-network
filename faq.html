What is ENM?
ENM (EnergieMind) is the utility token of the EnergieMIND energy intelligence ecosystem. It is an ERC-20 token on Ethereum Mainnet, designed for future ecosystem coordination related to energy intelligence, mining heat participation, pilot programs, and EnteleKRON mainnet alignment. ENM is a utility token — not an investment product.
What is the difference between enm.network and energiemind.com?
enm.network is the official ENM token documentation site covering the token layer: contract, tokenomics, presale, and risk disclosures. energiemind.com covers the EnergieMIND project layer: energy intelligence technology, mining heat utility, and infrastructure development. They are separate but connected parts of the same ecosystem.
