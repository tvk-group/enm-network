What is EnergieMIND?
EnergieMIND (ticker: ENM) is the utility token of the EnergieMIND energy intelligence ecosystem. It is an ERC-20 token on Ethereum Mainnet, designed for future ecosystem coordination related to energy intelligence, mining heat participation, pilot programs, and EnteleKRON mainnet alignment. ENM is a utility token — not an investment product.
What are the official EnergieMIND domains?
energiemind.com — main corporate/project website; energiemind.io — technology platform, dashboard, and app; energiemind.network — partners, nodes, miner-heaters, and energy network; energiemind.org — sustainability, research, and public mission; energiemind.shop — shop for miners, miner-heaters, solar, and energy products; enm.network — EnergieMIND (ENM), presale, and token documentation (this site).
